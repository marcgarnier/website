Research paper · COM 885

“That’s kino anon” — gamified reading charts on 4chan’s /lit/

A communication-research study of the “charts” on 4chan’s Literature board as gamified devices that frame and diffuse literary radicalism inside an anonymous counter-public. Corpus of 262 charts; four gamified mechanics and three framing structures.

Libertarianism in the age of platforms

A qualitative, comparative study of two libertarian YouTubers — Grand Angle and Dave Smith — and how they build visibility and credibility through formats, platform devices, discourse and self-presentation. Supervised by Sandrine Roginsky.
