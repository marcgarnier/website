Ongoing project · 2026 ongoing

The Informational Diet of /pol/ — what sources does 4chan's political board cite?

A computational audit of the news sources posted on 4chan's /pol/ board. Scrapes /pol/ periodically, extracts every external URL, classifies each source into five media categories (mainstream, alternative, state-funded, social media, institutional), and cross-tabulates by thread topic. Fully local, reproducible pipeline.
